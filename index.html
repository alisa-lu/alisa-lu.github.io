Meet Alisa!

Alisa is a second year computer science major at UC Irvine specializing in Information and minoring in Informatics.

Hello, I'm Alisa, and I use she/her pronouns! As a creative writer and programmer, I'm interested in creating enjoyable user experiences and incorporating storytelling in code, especially through design and interactive experiences. Currently, I am an undergraduate researcher at the the Advanced Power and Energy Program at UC Irvine, where I contribute code to renewable energy and connected and autonomous vehicle research. I am also the Secretary of Women in Information and Computer Sciences at UCI, and my previous experiences have been at IBM and Zotbins.
